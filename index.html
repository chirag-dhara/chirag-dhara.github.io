About

I am an interdisciplinary scientist currently serving as an Assistant Professor at Krea University[cite: 9, 10]. My career trajectory spans fundamental physics and earth system science, holding dual doctorates: a Ph.D. in Climate Science from the Max Planck Institute for Biogeochemistry (2017) and a Ph.D. in Quantum Information from the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (2013)[cite: 17, 18].

Prior to my current appointment, I served as a Research Associate at the Indian Institute of Tropical Meteorology (IITM) in Pune[cite: 11, 15]. My work is characterized by the application of rigorous physical principles to pressing global challenges in climate dynamics and sustainability[cite: 3, 4, 7].
